About Us

E-waste SmartHub envisions a world where individuals and businesses take responsibility of their E-waste and contribute towards a sustainable and environmental conscious Future. Our platform is dedicated to revolutionize the way E-waste is managed and recycled in India.

The platform provides seamless user-experience and aims to empower everyone to make Informed choices and promote efficient disposal of Electronic Waste.

We strive to be the catalyst for a paradigm shift in E-waste management, ensuring that no electronic device ends up in landfills or harms our ecosystems. Together, we can eliminate this alarming issue and create a greener Planet for our Future generations.
